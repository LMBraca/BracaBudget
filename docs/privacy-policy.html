Privacy Policy

Effective May 9, 2026

BracaBudget does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Overview

BracaBudget is a personal finance app developed by Luis Mario Bracamontes. This policy describes what data the app accesses and how it is handled.

The short version: we built the app to work entirely offline with no data collection. There are no accounts, no servers, and no third-party analytics.

Data we do not collect

BracaBudget does not collect:

  • Personal identifiable information
  • Financial account credentials
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Crash reports sent to any external service

Data stored on your device

All data you enter — transactions, categories, allocations, budget settings, preferences — is stored locally using Apple's SwiftData framework. It is never uploaded, never shared, and never leaves your device.

If you have iCloud Backup enabled, iOS may include this data in your device backup. That process is governed by Apple's privacy policy, not ours.

Exchange rate requests

When dual-currency mode is enabled, the app fetches live exchange rates from Frankfurter (frankfurter.dev). These requests send only the currency codes you selected (e.g., USD, MXN) — no personal data is included. Rates are cached on-device after the first fetch.

Siri & Shortcuts

The "Add Expense" Siri Shortcut is processed locally on your device by Apple's Siri infrastructure. We receive no data from these interactions.

Home screen widgets

Widgets display your weekly allowance using Apple's App Groups, a local on-device mechanism. No data is sent externally.

CSV export

Exported files are generated locally and shared through iOS's standard share sheet. We do not receive or access any exported data.

Data deletion

You can delete all app data at any time via Settings → Clear All Data. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

Children's privacy

Because no data is collected, the app is appropriate for all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page.
