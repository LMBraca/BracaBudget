How it works

BracaBudget is built around the envelope budgeting method — the proven strategy of giving every dollar a job before you spend it.

Set your monthly spending envelope, assign fixed allocations to recurring expenses (rent, groceries, transport), and BracaBudget calculates your exact weekly discretionary allowance. One number. Every week.
