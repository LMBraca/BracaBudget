Envelope budgeting for iOS.

Set your monthly spending envelope, assign fixed costs, and know exactly how much you can spend each week.

Free · iPhone · No account required

Features

  • Weekly allowance One clear number each week — what's left after fixed expenses are accounted for.
  • Allocations Set weekly or monthly caps per category. Visual progress bars warn you before you overspend.
  • Dual currency Budget in one currency, spend in another, with live exchange rates from Frankfurter.
  • Home screen widgets Small, medium, and large widgets showing your remaining allowance at a glance.
  • Siri Shortcuts Log expenses hands-free using the "Add Expense" shortcut.
  • Custom calendar Set a custom week start day and month start date to match your pay cycle.
  • CSV import/export Back up and restore your data in a portable, open format.
  • 100% local No account, no cloud, no ads. Your data never leaves your device.
